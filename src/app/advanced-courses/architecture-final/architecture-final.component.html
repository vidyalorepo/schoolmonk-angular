Introduction

Architecture is a culmination of art, science, and the ability to manage the construction of a building. Architects are in charge of designing homes, office spaces, educational institutions, and many more artificial marvels that we see today. When we were young, we built small house structures with playing cards. That’s when we unintentionally had a love for architecture. You need a creative and artistic mind to design compelling structures that look appealing to people. In the modern world, you should also keep in mind that your architectural design is environmentally friendly.

Architecture courses after 12th in India mainly focus on developing the skills needed to become an architect. The Architecture courses provides students with deep knowledge of building commercial buildings, airport terminals, highways construction, malls, complexes, and civil construction by applying basic concepts and innovative ideas.

Scope for architecture courses after 12th for Indian students

There are over 1 lakh architects in India, and the global architectural services market is expected to reach USD 344 billion in 2022, which determines the massive scope for architecture courses after 12th. The demand for architects is rising yearly as the real estate industry is booming at a higher pace than ever before.

The architecture course is about the study of designing and making buildings and structures. If you have an eye for design and a passion for architecture, you would be a perfect fit for this course. After completing this course, you can either work at architectural companies, independently as a freelance architect, or even start your own agency.

Let us now talk about the above-mentioned architecture courses after 12th in detail-

B. Arch

B.Arch is one of the most well-known architecture courses after 12th science. This course is mostly taken by students with a science background, however, some students with a design background also pursue it. B.Arch graduates are in high demand for jobs and career options in the work market, which primarily puts their talents and ideas to the test.

Eligibility: Eligibility for architecture after 12th is a minimum of 50% marks with PCM.

Duration: It is a 5-year course.

Institutes: IIT- Kharagpur, NIT- Calicut, SPA- Delhi, JMI- Delhi, etc. provide these architecture courses after 12th science.

B. Planning

Upon completing these architecture courses after 12th science students can work as city planners, regional planners, project planners, layout architects, and interior designers, among other fields. The curriculum is designed to answer how to become architect after 12th and prepare students for a research-based dissertation and to train them in a variety of design, engineering, management, and problem-solving skills.

Eligibility: Eligibility for architecture after 12th is a minimum of 50% marks in class 12 with PCM as subjects.

Duration: It is a 3-year course.

Institutes: These architecture courses after 12th science can be pursued from LPU- Jalandhar, COEP- Pune, SPA- Delhi, Amity- Mumbai, etc.

Diploma in Architectural Assistantship

The Architectural Assistantship Diploma program lasts three years. The candidate must be at least 15 years old to be considered for such architecture courses after 12th. These architecture courses after 12th are appropriate for individuals who have strong communication skills and can convey their thoughts both orally and in writing.

Eligibility: Eligibility for architecture after 12th is that students must have scored a minimum of 50% marks in class 12, in any stream.

Duration: It is a 3-year course

Institutes: Butler Polytechnic- Vadodara, CRRIT- Delhi, etc.

Diploma in Interior Design

A diploma in interior design answers how to become architect after 12th and teaches you how to assess a scene and picture it from many perspectives, how to interpret a colour theme, how to match colours to undertones, how to understand and properly fit furniture, and so on. The main benefit is that the course is short, yet it covers the essential principles of interior design and spatial organization.

Eligibility: A minimum of 50% marks in class 12, in any stream.

Duration: It is a 1-year course.

Institutes: AMU- Aligarh, Mumbai University, Poddar Group of Institutions- Jaipur, etc.

Certificate in Interior Design

Interior design technologies, colour theory, software design methodologies, and their implementations are all covered in these architecture courses after 12th. Business and residential design, software design tools, drawing abilities, as well as commercial, legal, moral, and other challenges that interior designers face are all covered.

Eligibility: Eligibility for architecture after 12th is 50% marks in class 12, in any stream.

Duration: It is a 6-month course.

Institutes: MFT- Chennai, Poddar Group of Institutions- Jaipur, GIFT- Ahmedabad, etc.

A profession in architecture is appealing, lucrative, and provides a lot of room for advancement. Architects can work as freelancers or on projects in addition to being affiliated with an organization. Specialization is beneficial for bettering one's chances in the field of architecture, as there are many factors to consider when developing a flawless model, both inside and out. Architects have a wide range of job titles for which they can be engaged. Some of the popular career prospects are landscape architects, industrial architects, commercial architects, freelance architects, urban planners, etc.

Over the last two decades, India's architecture sector has exploded. Various subdomains of architecture, such as town planning, urban planning, business centres, monumental buildings, and so on, are given a lot of attention. Architects have a lot of career prospects because commercial complexes and residential buildings are so important for day-to-day needs. Graduates are also hired for top public sector infrastructure projects such as roads construction, subway construction, and so on, with a competitive pay scale. The average starting salary for architects is 3-7 LPA, depending on the hiring firm and the possessed skillset.
