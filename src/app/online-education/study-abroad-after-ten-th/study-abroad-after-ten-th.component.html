Benefits of studying abroad after 10th grade

Benefits of studying abroad after 10th grade You can avail of many benefits by studying abroad. It can be a life-changing experience and can profit your career and life.

What are the requirements to study abroad after 10th grade?

To study abroad, you must keep a set of documents ready and handy. It is crucial to plan to get a seat in the best school. Here is what you need to know about the required documents.

1. Application Form- It is the first and foremost important document. An application form contains your personal and professional details. It is essential to fill the form with so much concern, and double-checking is mandatory before submitting it. It is also necessary to keep a soft copy of it.

2. English Tests- It is mandatory to clear the English language test. Only upon passing the exams, one can get admission to a school abroad. It is necessary to attach the scorecard with the application before sending it.

3. Essay- Some schools might ask you to write an essay, and by evaluating that, the members of the specific schools will select and offer you a seat. It serves as an opportunity for the students to showcase their talent and creative side.

4. Letter of Recommendation (LOR)- The letter of recommendation will act as an add-on for the admission team of a particular school to decide upon granting you the seat. So it is better to get a LOR stating your achievements, experience, skills, and contribution to the school or any social cause from your teachers or other staff.

5. Students Visa- To obtain a student visa, you must submit all the documentary evidence that you are well-sufficient to pay tuition, accommodation, and transport fees during your stay abroad.

Exams to be cleared

To get a seat abroad, one needs to clear a few exams. So it is mandatory to take this seriously and prepare for the tests. Some of the tests to appear after 10th grade are,

1. TOEFL- It is an English exam conducted by the Educational Testing Service (ETS). It is accepted worldwide in the US, USA, Australia, New Zealand, Europe, Asia, and Canada.

2. IELTS- It is an English exam conducted by IDP for international students who want to study in a country where English is the primary language of communication. The test is accepted in the UK, USA, New Zealand, Canada and Australia.

3. PTE - Exam is accepted at a few colleges in the US and most colleges in the UK. CAE and CPE are given preference by British universities. Further, PTE Academic scores are valid for up to two years from the date of the exam

Exams to Study Abroad

International students applying to foreign universities will have to take certain exams in order to be be eligible to study in these foreign countries. Read on the types of abroad exams that are mandatory for submission to study abroad.

Language exams

The language exams show your English proficiency skills. You need to give either one of the language exams to get admission to any university in an English-speaking country. These are required for pursuing almost all courses; Undergraduate courses, Postgraduate courses, Certificates or Diplomas (unless mentioned). Different countries give preference to one language exam over others. Few of the top English language exams which are used by prominent study-abroad destinations:

  • IELTS: IELTS exam is the preferred exam for getting admission to universities in the UK, Australia, and New Zealand. IELTS Score is valid for two years.
  • TOEFL: TOEFL exam is the preferred exam for universities in the US and Canada, while it is not accepted at many colleges in the UK. Your TOEFL scores are valid until two years post the declaration of your result.
  • PTE: PTE exam is accepted at a few colleges in the US and most colleges in the UK. CAE and CPE are given preference by British universities. Further, PTE Academic scores are valid for up to two years from the date of the exam.
